Available Ferret Rescues

These are ferrets that are in need of a new home

Sammy - Female

Sammy is a typical young ferret - full of energy and curiosity. She gets easily depressed if confined to her cage for long stretches of time and needs a loving and patient person (and business) welcome her as their own.

Maxwell - Male

Maxwell "Max" is as sweet as anything - bright eyed and inquisitive. Lots of energy and very loving. He loves stuffie toys and is a serial stasher. A little issue with toe nibbling but we are working on it.

Hunter - Male

Hunter is one of the sweetest ferrets you'll ever meet! He is super snuggly and loves to sit on your lap and be stroked while eating his breakfast. He is one of those pets that will always be by your side.

Cody - Male

Cody is a sweet little boy found wandering in someone's backyard and turned into FRES. He has a little issue with toe nibbling but we are working on it. He makes up for it by being very, very tidy with his litter habits.
